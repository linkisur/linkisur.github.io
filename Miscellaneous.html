Over the years, I have learned a valuable lesson: Engineering and Art are not separate. People always try and say the two are exclusive. I don't agree. Both are creative and leave people inspired. Film is a passion that has been sneaking around in my life for a long time. Only now am I realizing the things I can do with it.

Too Many Engineers- The problem with boats

Inspired by the Two Many Cooks video, created by Adult Swim, I decided to create my own version after a long night of working on my Robotics 101 class. It can be seen here

Babson Film Class: Gender Film Initiative

In a class taught by Kerry Rourke and Beth Wynstra, we developed a script, acted, and filmed our own films as groups. These films were produced to create an awareness for gender identity, and gender issues.

How To- Heat Exchanger.

A film developed do describe how a heat exchanger works. This was for a non-specified entry in my Thermodynamics class in the Spring 2015

Heat Exchanger

Edited, directed, and scripted by myself and Mindy Tieu, the math behind a project done for a non-specific entry for a portfolio

The Babson/Olin Interaction- The Snowman

A film developed produced in a Digital Documentary Class, taught at Olin College by Maruta Virtols

D4M4GED- The Material Science of Bleach

A film developed produced in a Material Science class as a supplement to a worksheet, taught at Olin College by Jon Stolk
