Olin College is a four-year college that specializes in creating an Engineer who can think for themselves and teach themselves how to learn. The project- based cirriculum promotes learning to function and work in groups.

Transport Project

Teams of 4-5 students chose an animal with which they need to design and build an experience for fourth grade students. Each group had to go through the different stages of deign, brainstorming, itteration, development, and creation. Our team got a Kangaroo and went to develop a game. It finally ended being a clue driven rescue mission, when the Momma Kangaroo had to find her baby joey. Balloons stood as dingos, which the momma kangaroo would punch to defend her baby.

Brainstorming

Many hours were spent brainstorming different games, mechanism designs, and goals for our group. We wanted to create a fun interactive game that woudl take into account the wants and wishes of our user-group.

Kangaroo

We developed a kangaroo, whose legs were spring loaded to mimick the kangaroo's natural jumping. A pole housed strings which students could pull to casue the kangaroo to punch. It was developed, designed, and created fully by our team. There was a heavy use of Solidworks

Game

We created a world that resembled different enviroments that could be found in Australia. There were six environments, each with a balloon that held clues to the location of the baby joey. (balloons are not shown)

Video Games for Software Deisgn

Two main projects for my Software Design Class were video games. I have decided to showcase two games I have partner programmed with others. These represent some of my first forays into python programming.

Space Team Down

This surival game was developed with gameplay that got more difficult as time progressed. With over 100 random weapons,a randomly generating map, a day and night cycle and crew members to keep alive, Space Team Down was a technological challenge for the team, as well as a fun game.I designed the artwork for our game, emulating old school pixel video games. During the process I helped design frameworks, story, and user interactions.

The World Ends With Coins

In this two player game, combatants are pitted against one another. They race to collect the most coins within the time limit. Developed in tandum with one other person, this interactive arcade style game is cute as well as fun!

bLOCK:POE Project

A smart bike lock that is easily opened with your android phone. Using the scrum methodology of iteration, the bike lock was developed in 8 weeks, during Olin's famed Principles of Engineering Class. For more information go to our final website here. This was a first pass at a concept of a bike lock.

Bike Lock

The final iteration of the bike lock for the class. This model made of pla, helped describe the concept.The bike lock design came from brainstorming sessions

Electrical Inside

Components were placed inside a custom designed case. Personally, I helped design the mechanical lock, assemble the product, and trouble shoot electrical issues.

Modeling Waves

Using MatLab and the beauty of differential equations, my partner on this project and I modeled waves.

Modeling of Put-Put Boat

Using MatLab. REFPROP, and knowledge learned on the interwebs, a team of studenst modeled and built a put-put boat. This was made for a final project in the Thermodynamics class- spring 2015

User Oriented Collaborative Design- Designing Boston's Future

In this second year design class Olin, my team and I worked a semester with the Tiny Home community. We went through three phases- Explore, Conceptualize, and Develop, to create a final design by the end of the semester. We developed a system to fit the values of the Tiny Home community by aligning them within the city of Boston.
